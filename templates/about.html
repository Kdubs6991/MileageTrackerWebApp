About The Mileage Tracker Project

This application is designed to help delivery drivers (DoorDash, UberEats etc.) track their weekly mileage and earnings efficiently. By keeping a consistent log, you can ensure you are maximizing your tax deductions at the end of the year.

Key Features

  • Weekly Logging: Track your miles and earnings week by week.
  • Smart Date Snapping: Select any day, and the app automatically assigns it to the correct week (Monday-Sunday).
  • Tax Estimates: Automatically calculates your estimated tax deduction based on the 2025 IRS Standard Mileage Rate ($0.70/mile).
  • Set Aside Calculator: Helps you budget for taxes by calculating 25% of your earnings.
  • Stride Import: Upload your CSV tax reports from Stride to automatically fill in your mileage.

How It Works

Simply go to the Add page to enter your data. You can manually type in your miles or upload a CSV file. The Home page gives you a dashboard view of your total earnings, miles, and potential tax deductions for the year.

Technologies Used

This project was built to replace a manual spreadsheet workflow, utilizing:

  • Backend: Python, Flask, SQLite
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Jinja2 Templating
  • Data Processing: Python CSV & IO Libraries
